## Energy ladder (motion policy as ceiling)

`motion policy` is a **ceiling** for how much happens in the shot. The server may refine policy from **music section** or **narrative intent**; treat that as authoritative.

- **static** — no voluntary subject locomotion; environment-only micro-motion. Camera only imperceptible drift or locked.
- **subtle** — one small gesture; micro-expression-level or weight transfer start.
- **moderate** — one clear, complete primary action; readable at normal viewing size.
- **dynamic** — strong, committed move; follow-through and reactive physics; camera may match energy.
- **frenetic** — sharp, high-speed primary beat; one accent only; avoid muddled multi-hit chaos.

**Rule of one**: the policy caps **how big** the move is, not how many *ideas* you pack in. One primary action, always.

**Resolve / outro** sections favor **deceleration and wider holds**; **climax / drop** favor a **single peak** not sustained chaos.
