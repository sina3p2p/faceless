## Phrase bank for `cameraMove`

The model only sees **one** `cameraMove` string. Use **type + direction + speed** (and lens feel when relevant), not story jargon.

- **Push / dolly in** — "slow dolly in along eyeline" · "chest-height push, subtle parallax on foreground"
- **Pull / dolly out** — "steady dolly back to reveal full environment"
- **Pan / tilt** — "slower pan right, eases in at the end" — keep horizon motivation clear
- **Whip** — "single horizontal whip, settles on a stable 2-beat hold"
- **Orbit** — "short arc, low radius, constant speed" — not a full 360 in one I2V beat unless policy is frenetic
- **Rack** — "rack focus from foreground specular to subject eyes"
- **Handheld** — "loose Z-axis drift, elbows weighted, not chaotic vibration"
- **Lock** — "locked tripod, imperceptible breathing only" when motion policy is static or subtle
- **Foreground pass** — "lateral dolly; foreground element wipes frame, subject tracks mid-ground"
- **Crane / jib (subtle)** — "slow rise or fall 6–12 inches, horizon stays level" — for hero reveals, not stunts
- **Establishing sweep** — "wide lock or slow 10° pan, three depth planes read"
- **OTS drift** — "over-the-shoulder, 2–3 inch lateral drift" — for dialogue/connection beats (no lip-sync in motion spec unless the frame supports it)
- **Documentary follow** — "1–2 ft trailing offset, same walking cadence" — for chase/walk, one axis dominant

**Anti-patterns**: "cinematic" without mechanics; "dramatic" without move type; two incompatible moves in one line.
